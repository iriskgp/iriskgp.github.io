About the Institute

The Institute of Research and Innovation in Science (IRIS), Kharagpur, is founded on the belief that rigorous inquiry and technological creativity together can transform society. At IRIS, research is guided by curiosity and a commitment to excellence, encouraging scholars to explore ideas beyond conventional boundaries. The institute fosters an environment where interdisciplinary collaboration thrives, enabling innovations that are both scientifically robust and socially relevant. Through its academic programs and research initiatives, IRIS seeks not only to expand the frontiers of knowledge but also to nurture thoughtful, responsible leaders in science and technology.

Research Thrust Areas

Our research is organized into six strategic areas, ensuring focused expertise and synergistic interdisciplinary discovery.

Machine Learning & AI

Deep Learning, Interpretability, Decision Systems.

Signal Processing

Spectral Analysis, Imaging, Biomedical Signals.

Embedded Systems

RTOS, Low-Power Computing, SoC Architecture.


Power Electronics

Smart Grids, Renewable Energy Integration, Converters.

Internet of Things (IoT)

Sensor Networks, Edge Computing, Protocols.

Algorithms & Theory

Complexity, Optimization, Randomized Methods.
